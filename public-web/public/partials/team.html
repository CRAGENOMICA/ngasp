The Team


This is a multidisciplinar team where population geneticist, computer enginners, theoretical physicist, bioinformatics and molecular biologist are interacting to develop ngasp software. The team is coordinated from the CRAG centre and collaborates with a number of different institutions. The project is funded by the Ministerio de Economia y Competitividad (MEC, Spain).

We are focused in making an efficient and user friendly plattform to estimate accurately the levels and patterns of variation from different NGS datasets (haploid, diploid, pooled data) at populations.
